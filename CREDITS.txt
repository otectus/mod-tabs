The nearby-container tab feature (one tab per chest/barrel/shulker/modded
inventory in a radius around the player, with double-chest dedup and the
synthetic right-click open path) is a clean-room implementation inspired by
the design of "InventoryTabs" by dhyces / Kavin Phan and earlier authors —
https://github.com/dhyces/InventoryTabs — licensed under the MIT License.
No source code was copied; the underlying ideas (radius scan, useItemOn
right-click forwarding, double-chest half merging) are credited here.
